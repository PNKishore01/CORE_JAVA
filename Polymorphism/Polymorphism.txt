Ploymorphism :-
* It is representing one form into many forms in java one form is actual or original method and many forms 
are known as overloaded or overrided methods.

* Ploymorphism is two types:
    1. Compile_time(overloading), 2.Run-time(overriding)

1. Compile_time is also known as early binding, static Ploymorphism or method overloading
    * when we have two or more methods with same name with different parameters irrespective of return types within a
    class is known as method overloading.
2. Run_time is also known as late binding, dynamic Ploymorphism or method overriding
    * when we have two or more methods with same name with same parameters, same return types having parent-child
    class relation is known as method overriding.
